Le Dep is a 2015 Canadian psychological drama film directed by Sonia Boileau . 

The film tells the story of a young Innu woman ( played by Ãˆve Ringuette ) who is held at gunpoint one night while working at a convenience store in a small First - Nations community in rural Quebec . 

Set in a fictional Innu community , the film 's dialogue is mostly in French , with some Innu - aimun . 

Le Dep is the first First Nations production of Telefilm Canada 's Micro - Budget program . 

The film 's world premiere was at the 2015 Karlovy Vary International Film Festival , following which the film played various festivals in Canada , the United States , and United Kingdom and had a theatrical run in Montreal .
