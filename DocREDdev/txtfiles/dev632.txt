He was born in Brooklyn , New York and became a feature writer with the New York World - Telegram . 

Among his books , Elliott Arnold is probably best known for his 1947 novel Blood Brother that was adapted as the acclaimed 1950 motion picture Broken Arrow and a 1956 TV series of the same name . 

The popular Indian Wedding Blessing is based on a passage from Blood Brother . 

His 1949 biography of Sigmund Romberg was made into the 1954 musical film , Deep in My Heart . 

Elliott Arnold died in New York City in 1980 at the age of sixty - seven .
