William César de Oliveira ( born 17 October 1968 ) is a former Brazilian international footballer . 

He has played for several domestic club teams in Brazil such as Vasco Esporte Clube , Flamengo , Fluminense FC , Clube Atlético Mineiro etc . 

William César de Oliveira was part of the Brazilian U17 football team at the inaugural edition of the FIFA U-16 World Championship ( now known as FIFA U17 World Cup ) in 1985 . 

He played a vital role in Brazil 's third place finish at the first edition of the Youth FIFA World Cup with scoring 5 goals in the entire tournament . 

He was awarded the Golden Ball at the 1985 World Cup as he was the second leading goal scorer in the tournament just behind Marcel Witeczek of Germany .
