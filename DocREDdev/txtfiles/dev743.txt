Jonathan Joss ( born December 22 , 1965 ) is an American actor . 

He is best known for portraying the voice of John Redcorn from season 2 to 13 of the animated series King of the Hill , and for his recurring role as Chief Ken Hotate in Parks and Recreation . 

Born in San Antonio , Texas , Joss is of Spanish , Comanche and White Mountain Apache descent .

Joss is also a musician , and performs as part of the Red Corn Band , an alternative blues group which has won a Grammy Award for Best Native American Music Album .
