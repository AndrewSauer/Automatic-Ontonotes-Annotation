Isle of Palms is a city in Charleston County , South Carolina , United States . 

At the 2010 census , the population was 4,133 . 

Isle of Palms is a barrier island on the South Carolina coast . 

The city is included within the Charleston - North Charleston - Summerville metropolitan area and the Charleston - North Charleston Urbanized Area . 

The town lies along a narrow strip of land , hugging the beach , separated from the mainland by the Intracoastal Waterway . 

It is an affluent community of both vacation home owners and year - round residents , with large beachfront homes , resorts , and local restaurants . 

Beach volleyball is popular in the summer , and the " Windjammer " club hosts several tournaments throughout the year .
