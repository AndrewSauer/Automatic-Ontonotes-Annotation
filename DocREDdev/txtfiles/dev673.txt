Pavol Sedlák ( born 21 November 1979 in Bratislava ) is a Slovak footballer , who currently plays for SK Schwadorf and also assistant manager in the Slovak giant ŠK Slovan Bratislava . 

His former clubs were MŠK Rimavská Sobota and ŠK Slovan Bratislava . 

He previously played for FK DAC 1904 Dunajská Streda . 

He is a midfielder with a lot of international experience . 

He is well known for his dribbling ability and super strong shots from medium distances . 

Together with Samuel Slovák , he is one of the leaders inside the squad of the club .
