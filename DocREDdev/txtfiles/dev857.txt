How Could Hell Be Any Worse ? 

is the first full - length album by American punk rock band Bad Religion , released on January 19 , 1982 by Epitaph Records . 

Released almost a year after their self - titled EP , it was financed by a $ 3,000 loan by guitarist Brett Gurewitz 's father . 

Its success surprised the band when it sold 10,000 copies in under a year .
