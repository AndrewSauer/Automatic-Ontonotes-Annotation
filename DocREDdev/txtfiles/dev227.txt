McLamore attended Northfield Mount Hermon School before matriculating at Cornell University . 

McLamore was an employee and also a businessman before . 

Edgerton originally opened Insta Burger King in Miami , Florida on March 1 , 1954 . 

Three months later , on June 1 , he met McLamore and they formed the Burger King Corporation . 

The corporation opened Burger King stores and went on to introduce the Whopper burger in 1957 , when it also dropped " Insta " from the name . 

The pair sold the business to Pillsbury in 1967 and McLamore served as Burger King 's president until 1970 , and was chairman to 1976 . 

McLamore died of cancer in Coral Gables , Florida on August 9 , 1996 , at the age of 70 .
