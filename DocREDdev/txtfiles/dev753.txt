Jerry Steiner ( January 7 , 1918 – February 1 , 2012 ) was an American professional basketball player . 

He played two seasons in the National Basketball League ( NBL ) , one of the two leagues that merged to form the National Basketball Association . 

Steiner , a 5'7 " point guard was a basketball player for Butler University from 1937 to 1940 . 

He made the 1940 All - American team as a senior for the Bulldogs . 

After graduating from Butler , Steiner played for one season for the Indianapolis Kautskys of the NBL for the 1945–46 season . 

After serving in World War II until 1946 , Steiner took a job teaching and coaching at Shortridge High School in Indianapolis while playing for the Fort Wayne Zollner Pistons . 

He left the game after the 1946–47 season . 

Steiner died on February 1 , 2012 , in Bonita Springs , Florida .
