House of Angels ( ) is a Swedish drama film which was released to cinemas in Sweden on 21 February 1992 , about a little village in Västergötland , Sweden , where an aging recluse lives in a mansion on a large wooded property . 

One day he is accidentally killed and an unknown relative by the name of Fanny Zander inherits the mansion and land . 

When she and her friend Zac arrive , they turn life in the staid village upside down . 

The film was screened out of competition at the 1992 Cannes Film Festival . 

At the 28th Guldbagge Awards the film won the awards for Best Film and Best Director . 

It was also nominated for Best Actress ( Helena Bergström ) , Best Screenplay and Best Cinematography ( Jens Fischer ) . 

The film was selected as the Swedish entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 65th Academy Awards , but was not accepted as a nominee . 

A sequel , Änglagård – andra sommaren , was produced in 1994 . 

A second sequel , Änglagård – tredje gången gillt , was released on DVD and Blu - ray on 25 May 2011 .
