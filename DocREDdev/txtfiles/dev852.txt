Bone Machine is the tenth studio album by Tom Waits , released in 1992 on Island Records . 

It won a Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album , and features guest appearances by Los Lobos ' David Hidalgo , Primus ' Les Claypool and Brain , and The Rolling Stones ' Keith Richards . 

Bone Machine marked a return to studio material for Waits , coming a full five years after his previous studio album , Franks Wild Years ( 1987 ) . 

The album is often noted for its dark lyrical themes of death and murder , and for its rough , stripped - down , percussion - heavy blues rock style . 

Bone Machine was included on many Best Albums of the 1990s lists , including Pitchfork where it was number 49 , and Rolling Stone where it was number 53 .
