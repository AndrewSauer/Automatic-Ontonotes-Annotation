Volcanoes Stadium is a minor league baseball park in the northwest United States , located in Keizer , Oregon . 

It is the home field of the Salem - Keizer Volcanoes , a Class A affiliate of the San Francisco Giants in the short - season Northwest League . 

Nicknamed " Oregon 's Field of Dreams " , it opened in 1997 and has a capacity of 4,254 people . 

The ballpark is adjacent to Interstate 5 , just beyond the right field fence , and sits at an approximate elevation of above sea level . 

The Volcanoes have won five Northwest League championships , in 1998 , 2001 , 2006 , 2007 , and 2009 . 

The team moved to Salem - Keizer in 1997 , after two seasons in Bellingham , Washington , preceded by eleven years in Everett . 

Salem 's previous NWL teams in the 1980s played at Chemeketa Community College .
