Inner Life was an American club - oriented soul studio project formed in 1979 that enjoyed success with " I 'm Caught Up ( In a One Night Love Affair ) " ( 1979 ) and " Ai n't No Mountain High Enough " ( 1981 ) . 

Both tracks featured the vocals of Jocelyn Brown and Leroy Burgess ( formerly of Black Ivory ) . 

Produced and arranged by Patrick Adams and engineered by Bob Blank and Joe Arlotta , the album was originally produced for executive producer Greg Carmichael , whose Red Greg Records was an early and prolific disco label based in New York . 

They also had a minor hit on Salsoul Records in 1981 with " Knock Out ( Let 's Go Another Round ) " .
