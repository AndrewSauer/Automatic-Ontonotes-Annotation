The List of diplomats of Norway to China began when diplomatic relations were established in 1851 . 

The establishment of diplomatic relations took place during the union between Sweden and Norway , and a Norwegian - Swedish consulate general was established in Guangzhou . 

A vice consulate was set up in Shanghai in 1853 . 

When the vice consulate in Shanghai became the consulate general in 1863 , and the consulate general in Guangzhou was upgraded to a vice consulate . 

When the union of Norway and Sweden was ended in 1905 , Norway sent the consul - general , Thorvald Hansen , to Shanghai .
