The Mudlark is a 1950 film made in Britain by 20th Century Fox . 

It is a fictional account of how Queen Victoria was eventually brought out of her mourning for her dead husband , Prince Albert . 

It was directed by Jean Negulesco , written and produced by Nunnally Johnson and based on the 1949 novel of the same name by American artillery sergeant and San Francisco newspaperman Theodore Bonnet ( 1908 â€“ 1983 ) . 

It stars Irene Dunne , Alec Guinness and Andrew Ray . 

" Mudlarks " were street children who survived by scavenging and selling what they could find on the banks of the River Thames . 

The film was a hit in Britain and made an overnight star of Andrew Ray , who played the title character .
