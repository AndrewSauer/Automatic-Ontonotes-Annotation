Furse , born 20 April 1880 in South Carolina , was a member of the United States Naval Academy class of 1901 . 

His first service was on the Asiatic Station , where he served in Manila during a scientific expedition , as well as in other ships . 

Returning to the United States , he joined Illinois ( BB-7 ) 29 September 1904 , and in her served in Cuban waters . 

Lieutenant Furse died on board Illinois 30 September 1907 , of injuries received fighting a storm which threatened his ship .
