The Metacomet Ridge , Metacomet Ridge Mountains , or Metacomet Range of southern New England is a narrow and steep fault - block mountain ridge known for its extensive cliff faces , scenic vistas , microclimate ecosystems , and rare or endangered plants . 

The ridge is an important recreation resource located within of more than 1.5 million people , offering four long - distance hiking trails and over a dozen parks and recreation areas , including several historic sites . 

It has been the focus of ongoing conservation efforts because of its natural , historic , and recreational value , involving municipal , state , and national agencies and nearly two dozen non - profit organizations .
