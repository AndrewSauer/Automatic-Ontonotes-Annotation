The Expendables is an American series of ensemble action films written by and starring Sylvester Stallone and originally created by David Callaham . 

The film series itself was created to pay homage to the blockbuster action films of the 1980s and ' 90s and also pays gratitude to the action stars of those decades , as well as more recent stars in action . 

The series consists of three films : The Expendables ( 2010 ) , The Expendables 2 ( 2012 ) and The Expendables 3 ( 2014 ) , with a final fourth film scheduled to be released in the future . 

The series has received mixed critical reception , in regard to its plots and dialogue between the characters ; however , many critics praised the use of humor and action scenes . 

The films have been box office successes .
