La prima notte di quiete , released in USA as Indian Summer ( from the phrase Der Nachsommer , an insignia of Goethe ) is a 1972 Italian and French drama film directed by Valerio Zurlini . 

It is starred and produced by Alain Delon . 

In France and Germany it runs less than the Italian cut . 

It is known and available on DVD in France as Le Professeur ( The Professor ) and in Germany as Oktober in Rimini ( October in Rimini ) . 

It tells the story of a gifted but restless young man who can not live up to the high traditions of his family and survives in obscurity , taking casual teaching jobs and living in a loveless partnership with a depressed woman . 

His aimless existence reaches a crisis point when he falls for one of his pupils , a beautiful but badly damaged girl .
