Tippy Walker ( born February 19 , 1947 ) is a former American child actress , best known for her role in the film The World of Henry Orient ( 1964 ) . 

Born Elizabeth Tipton Walker in New York City , her father was an engineer with Allied Chemical Corporation . 

She attended The Masters School in Dobbs Ferry , New York . 

After appearing in several television shows , such as Doctor Kildare and Peyton Place , and the female lead role in the film Jennifer on My Mind ( which featured a young Robert De Niro ) , she retired from acting and opened an art gallery called " Out Of The Blue " , near the Yale University campus . 

In 1984 she moved to New Haven , Connecticut .
