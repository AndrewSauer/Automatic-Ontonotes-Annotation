Marlow was a left - handed batsman who bowled left - arm slow - medium . 

He was born at Anstey , Leicestershire . 

Marlow joined the Leicestershire ground staff in 1898 , and initially played in the second team . 

He made his first - class debut against Sussex in the 1900 County Championship at Grace Road . 

Marlow made fourteen further first - class appearances for the county , the last of which came against Essex in the 1903 County Championship . 

In his total of fifteen first - class matches , he took 31 wickets at an average of 27.29 , with best figures of 6/50 . 

One of two five wicket hauls he took , his best figures came against Hampshire in the 1902 County Championship . 

A poor tailend batsman , Marlow scored 46 runs at a batting average of 3.28 . 

He died at Leicester , Leicestershire on 13 August 1954 .
