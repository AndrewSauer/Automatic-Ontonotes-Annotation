Sandra is a female name , which is often used as a short form for Alexandra or Cassandra . 

Alexandra is a feminine form of the male name Alexander , which is a romanization of the Greek name Ἀλέξανδρος Alexandros . 

It is generally interpreted to mean " protector of man " or " defender of man " . 

The name Cassandra is also from the Greek (: " she who entangles men " ) . 

Cassandra is known in Greek mythology , as the daughter of King Priam and Queen Hecuba of Troy . 

She was loved by Apollo and given the gift of prophecy but when she did not fall in love with him , he placed a curse on her so that no one would believe her predictions .
