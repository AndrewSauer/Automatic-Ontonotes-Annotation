Hu Ge ( , born 20 September 1982 ) is a Chinese actor and singer . 

While studying at the Shanghai Theater Academy , he was invited to play the leading role Li Xiaoyao in the 2005 television series Chinese Paladin , and immediately skyrocketed to fame as one of China 's most popular actors . 

In September 2012 , he was nominated for Best Newcomer at the 31st Hundred Flowers Awards for his role as Lin Juemin in Chinese historical film 1911 . 

He is best known for his role as Mei Changsu in the 2015 television drama Nirvana in Fire , for which he received the Best Actor Award at the 22nd Magnolia Awards . 

Hu Ge is currently managed by Hu Ge Studio , contracted under Tangren Media .
