Yousef Mangoush , born in Benghazi , Cyrenaica , Libya , in 1950 , is a Major General . 

He was named as the Chief of Staff of the Libyan Ground Forces on 2 January 2012 , Mangoush served in the Libyan army under the Gaddafi government and retired in 1999 at the rank of Colonel . 

He had been a special forces commander in the Libyan Army prior to his retirement . 

Mangush was the deputy defense minister in the interim government of Prime Minister Abdurrahim El - Keib . 

Mangoush joined the rebel forces in February 2011 during the Libyan conflict which toppled Muammar Gaddafi , Mangush was arrested in the oil town of Brega in April by Gaddafi 's forces and freed in late August following the fall of Tripoli .
