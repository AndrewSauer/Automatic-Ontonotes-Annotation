Tenglong Cave ( ) is a cave located from Lichuan City , Hubei , China . 

It is believed to be the longest monomer karst cave system in the world . 

The cave entrance is and wide , leading to of passageways . 

An underground network of streams runs for whilst the cave is the source of the Qingjiang River . 

Year round temperatures underground remain in the 16–18 degrees Celsius range . 

To facilitate tourist access to the cave , as well as to the so - called Enshi Grand Canyon Scenic Area ( 恩施大峡谷景区 ) , the prefectural authorities are considering plans for the construction of a tourist railway , which will link these two popular tourist attractions with a station on the Yichang−Wanzhou Railway ( probably , Lichuan ) .
