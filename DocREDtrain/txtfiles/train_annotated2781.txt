Ayeta Anne Wangusa , born in Kampala , Uganda , on 9 September 1971 , is a writer and activist . 

A founding member ( 1995 ) of FEMRITE , the Uganda Women Writers Association , Wangusa first achieved broader recognition in literary circles for her novel Memoirs of a Mother ( 1998 ) . 

She was also a founding member ( 2009 ) of the African Writers Trust , currently ( as of 2018 ) serving on the advisory board .
