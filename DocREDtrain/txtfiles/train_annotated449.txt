Christine Kenneally ( born in Melbourne , Australia ) is an Australian - American journalist who writes on science , language and culture . 

Trained as a linguist , she has written for The New York Times , the New Yorker , Slate , New Scientist , and Australia 's Monthly , among other publications . 

She is a great - granddaughter of JJ Kenneally , an early popularizer of Australian bushranger Ned Kelly . 

Her first book , The First Word ( 2007 ) was a L.A. Times book prize finalist and has been translated into Korean and Spanish . 

Her book for Viking Penguin , The Invisible History of the Human Race : How DNA and History Shape Our Identities and Our Futures , was released 9 October 2015 .
