Hercules & Love Affair is a dance music project created by American DJ Andy Butler in 2004 . 

Consisting of a rotating cast of performers and musicians , the band work within the genres of house music , disco , techno and nu - disco . 

Originally based in New York City , now based in Ghent , Belgium , Hercules and Love Affair were founded following Butler 's collaboration with Anohni to produce the song " Blind " , which was a hit single . 

Initially signing to DFA Records , for their eponymous 2008 debut album , the band went on to record two more albums for Moshi Moshi , Blue Songs ( 2011 ) and The Feast of the Broken Heart ( 2014 ) . 

Their fourth album Omnion was released in September 2017 by Atlantic Records .
