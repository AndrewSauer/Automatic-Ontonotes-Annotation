Tetlin Junction ( also Forty Mile , Tetlin Center , or Tetling Junction ) is an unincorporated community in Southeast Fairbanks Census Area , Alaska , United States . 

Its elevation is 1,745   feet ( 532   m ) , along the north bank of the Tanana River . 

The official spelling of its name has been " Tetlin Junction " since a Board on Geographic Names ruling in 1950 . 

The community is named for its location at the junction of the Alaska and Taylor highways : it lies 8   miles ( 13   km ) east of Tok Junction , 13   miles ( 21   km ) north - northwest of Tetlin , and 11   miles ( 18   km ) east - southeast of Tanacross .
