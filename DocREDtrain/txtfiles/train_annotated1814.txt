Saints and Sinners is an album by American rock band Young Dubliners . 

The album was released on February 3 , 2009 . 

The album is their first effort with all original material ( their previous release , With All Due Respect , was essentially a tribute to Irish music ) . 

It features guest appearances by Kenny Wayne Shepherd , Eric Rigler and Cindy Wasserman . 

The first single , " Rosie " , gained considerable airplay on U.S. radio stations , as did " Buy You a Life " . 

The band performed " Rosie " on ABC TV 's Jimmy Kimmel Live ! 

on March 17 , 2009 ; they also appeared on the show on March 17 , 2011 and played " Howaya Girls " .
