The Carolina Thunderbirds were a professional ice hockey team located in Winston - Salem , North Carolina , United States . 

The Thunderbirds played their home games at the old Winston - Salem Memorial Coliseum before the arena was demolished in 1989 . 

The team played in the Atlantic Coast Hockey League from 1981 to 1987 , the All - American Hockey League during 1987–88 and finally moved into the newly created East Coast Hockey League in 1988 . 

The Carolina Thunderbirds were one of five teams that played during the inaugural season of East Coast Hockey League ( ECHL ) . 

The Thunderbirds won the first ever ECHL championship and were awarded the Riley Cup for the 1988–89 ECHL season . 

The team changed its name to the Winston - Salem Thunderbirds before the start of 1989–90 season and the team remained in the ECHL until the end of the 1991–92 season when it was announced by co - owner Ed Broyhill that Winston - Salem would move to West Virginia to play as the Wheeling Thunderbirds .
