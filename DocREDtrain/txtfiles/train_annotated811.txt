Lake County is a county in the south - central region of the U.S. state of Oregon . 

As of the 2010 census , the population was 7,895 . 

Its county seat is Lakeview . 

The county is named after the many lakes found within its boundaries , including Lake Abert , Summer Lake , Hart Lake , and Goose Lake . 

Lake County is in the high desert region known as the Oregon Outback , on the northwestern edge of the Great Basin . 

The county is generally divided between the communities around Lakeview and Paisley to the south and the communities around Christmas Valley , Fort Rock , and Silver Lake to the north . 

Its economy consists largely of agriculture and natural resource management and extraction . 

It is home to many large cattle ranches , hay farms , and timber holdings ( both public and private ) , as well as several frontier towns and early 20th - century homesteads . 

Although lumber was once a primary economic driver in Lake County , today only one mill remains , at Lakeview .
