Southside is the debut studio album by American R&B ; recording artist Lloyd . 

It was released on July 20 , 2004 , by The Inc. Records and Def Jam Recordings ( see 2004 in music ) . 

The singer teamed with several of record producers such as Chink Santana , Rodney Jerkins , Irv Gotti and Jasper Cameron , among others . 

The album garnered a mostly positive reception from music critics , and produced the successful top thirty Billboard 200 hit , " Southside " with label - mate Ashanti and " Hey Young Girl " which failed chart or sell noticeably . 

Soon considered a commercial failure , it reached the top ten of U.S. 

Billboard 's Top R&B;/Hip - Hop Albums chart at number three , and number eleven on the Billboard 200 , selling over 67,000 copies in its first week .
