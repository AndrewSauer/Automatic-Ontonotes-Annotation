" Déjà Vu " is a hit 1979 ballad song written by Isaac Hayes and Adrienne Anderson and recorded by Dionne Warwick in 1979 . 

It was the second single released from her Arista debut album , Dionne , which was produced by Barry Manilow . 

The song was Warwick 's fifth and last top 40 single of her 1970s period and her second top 40 single following the release of " I 'll Never Love This Way Again " in the five years since her number - one single , " Then Came You " , featuring The Spinners . 

" Déjà Vu " went to number 15 on the Billboard Hot 100 , number 25 on the soul chart , and number one on the Adult Contemporary chart in early 1980 . 

Warwick won the Grammy for Best Female R&B ; Vocal Performance for " Déjà Vu " .
