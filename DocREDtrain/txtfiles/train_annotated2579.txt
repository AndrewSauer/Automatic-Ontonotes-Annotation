Societatea Națională de Gaze Naturale Romgaz SA Mediaș ( SNGN Romgaz SA ) or simply Romgaz is the largest natural gas producer in Romania and one of the largest in Eastern Europe . 

The company is responsible for producing around 40 % of the total natural gas consumption in Romania . 

Its majority stockholder is the Government of Romania , which owns 70.01 % . 

Fondul Proprietatea owns 14.99 % , while the remaining 15 % is owned by various shareholders . 

The company is specialised in geological research for the discovery of hydrocarbons , production , storage , commercialization and the supply of natural gas and natural gas condensate . 

Romgaz is structured into six branches : two production branches located in Târgu Mureș and Mediaș , one underground storage branch located in Ploieşti , a special operations branch located in Mediaş , a maintenance branch located in Târgu Mureș and one international office located in Bratislava , Slovakia .
