Owned and bred by Queen Elizabeth II , was undefeated in her three races as a two - year - old in 1981 , winning the Acomb Stakes , May Hill Stakes and Fillies ' Mile . 

In the following year she added a win in the Lupe Stakes before a record - breaking victory in the Princess of Wales 's Stakes . 

She ran poorly in her two remaining races and was retired to stud at the end of the season . 

Height of Fashion proved to be an exceptional broodmare , producing the major stakes winners Unfuwain , Nashwan and Nayef . 

She died in Kentucky in 2000 .
