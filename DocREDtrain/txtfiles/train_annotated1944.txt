FBOP Corporation was a financial services company based in Oak Park , Illinois , United States . 

As of mid-2009 , it had $ 18.5 billion in assets and was the 46th largest bank holding company in the United States . 

On October 30 , 2009 , FBOP 's banking subsidiaries were closed by their chartering agencies and the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation was appointed as their receiver . 

The company had over 4064 employees . 

The holding company began as First Bank of Oak Park . 

FBOP started acquiring other banks in 1990 . 

In 2006 , First Bank of Oak Park merged with four other co - owned banks in Illinois to create Park National Bank . 

FBOP operated banks in Illinois , California , Texas , and Arizona , prior to their closure . 

U.S. Bancorp acquired all nine of FBOP 's nine banks on the day of closure , but later sold the three Texas - based banks to Prosperity Bancshares .
