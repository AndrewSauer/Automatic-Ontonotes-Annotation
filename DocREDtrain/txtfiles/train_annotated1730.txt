Comal County ( ) is a county located on the Edwards Plateau in the U.S. state of Texas . 

As of the 2010 census , its population was 108,472 . 

Its county seat is New Braunfels . 

Comal County is part of the San Antonio - New Braunfels , TX Metropolitan Statistical Area . 

Along with Hays and Kendall counties , Comal was listed in 2017 of the nation 's ten fastest - growing large counties with a population of at least ten thousand .
