The Chongqing West railway station is a railway station of Chengyu Passenger Railway that is located in Shapingba District of Chongqing , People 's Republic of China . 

It is the second largest railway station , after Xi'an North , in South Western China when opened on January 25th , 2018 . 

It will serve as Chongqing 's connection with many destinations across south - western and north - western China . 

Situated in Shangqiao area of Shapingba , near the intersections of the G93 Chengdu – Chongqing Ring Expressway and the G85 Chongqing – Kunming Expressways . 

This is the third major railway station to serve Chongqing , after Chongqing North and Chongqing Stations . 

It opened in conjunction with the smaller nearby Shapingba station on the same day . 

It will serve as a stop on the Chongqing – Lanzhou High - Speed Railway , Xiangyang - Chongqing Railway , Chongqing – Guizhou High - Speed Railway , Chongqing - Kunming Railway and Chengdu – Chongqing Railway .
