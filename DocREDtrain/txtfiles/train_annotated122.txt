The Thomas Viaduct spans the Patapsco River and Patapsco Valley between Relay , Maryland and Elkridge , Maryland , USA . 

It was commissioned by the Baltimore and Ohio Railroad ( B&O ; ) ; built between July 4 , 1833 , and July 4 , 1835 ; and named for Philip E. Thomas , the company 's first president . 

At its completion , the Thomas Viaduct was the largest railroad bridge in the United States and the country 's first multi - span masonry railroad bridge to be built on a curve . 

It remains the world 's oldest multiple arched stone railroad bridge . 

In 1964 , it was designated as a National Historic Landmark . 

The viaduct is now owned and operated by CSX Transportation and still in use today , making it one of the oldest railroad bridges still in service .
