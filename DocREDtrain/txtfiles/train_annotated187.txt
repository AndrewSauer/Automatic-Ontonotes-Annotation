Petre Grosu ( born 27 November 1955 ) is a Romanian former professional footballer who played as a midfielder or as a forward . 

He started his professional career at Sportul Studențesc , but he would have known full glory at Bihor Oradea being the top scorer of the team in the 1981–82 Divizia B season , also succeeding in promoting with Bihor Oradea . 

The best performance would come at the end of the 1982 – 83 Divizia A season when Grosu has become the top scorer of the Divizia A , the first and the last in the history of FC Bihor . 

With this performance , he secured a place in the Bihor Oradea hall of fame and a well - established place in the memory of supporters .
