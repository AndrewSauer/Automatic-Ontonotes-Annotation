Bailey dominated Connecticut Democratic politics as a party chairman , from 1946 to his death in 1975 . 

He typically had a decisive voice in selecting the party 's candidates for top offices and in coordinating Democrats in the state legislature . 

He was even more powerful as the chairman of the Democratic National Committee from 1961 until 1968 , and was one of the main behind - the - scenes backers of John F. Kennedy . 

An Irish Catholic , Bailey was educated at The Catholic University of America and Harvard Law School .
