" Yearning for Your Love " is a ballad recorded and released by The Gap Band on Mercury Records . 

The single was the third release off the band 's fifth album , The Gap Band III . 

Four different singles , each with a different B - side , were released in 1981 . 

The song became a modest hit on the US and UK charts when it was released ; it arose to number five on the Hot Soul Singles chart and number sixty on the Billboard Hot 100 . 

Written by Gap Band backing member / keyboardist Oliver Scott and keyboardist Ronnie Wilson , it was a romantic love song dedicated to Wilson 's wife at the time and was performed by Ronnie 's brother Charlie Wilson .
