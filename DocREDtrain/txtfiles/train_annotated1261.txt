William Paul " Bill " Cole III ( born May 16 , 1956 ) is an American businessman , politician and a former Republican member of the West Virginia Senate , representing the 6th district from 2013 to 2017 . 

He was the President of the Senate ( and thus first in line to the governorship ) from 2015 to 2017 . 

Cole also served in the West Virginia House of Delegates from May 28 , 2010 , to January 12 , 2011 , to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Delegate John Shott . 

Cole was the Republican nominee for West Virginia Governor in 2016 , choosing to run for the office rather than run for re - election to the state senate . 

He lost the election to Jim Justice and was succeeded by Mitch Carmichael as Senate President .
