I Am Ready is American country music artist Steve Wariner 's tenth full - length album . 

It was released in 1991 on Arista Records as his first for the label , following his departure from MCA Records . 

Singles from the album include " Leave Him Out of This " , " The Tips of My Fingers " ( a cover of Bill Anderson 's 1960 hit ) , " A Woman Loves " , " Crash Course in the Blues " and " Like a River to the Sea " . 

Respectively , these reached # 6 , # 3 , # 9 , # 32 and # 30 on the Billboard country charts . 

The album was certified gold by the RIAA , and was Wariner 's first album to achieve that certification .
