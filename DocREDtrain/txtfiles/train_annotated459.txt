Henrik Malmström ( born August 26 , 1978 in Kristianstad ) is a retired Swedish professional ice hockey forward who last played for Sparta Warriors of the GET - ligaen in Norway . 

Malmström began his career in the Swedish HockeyAllsvenskan , the country 's second tier league , playing for his hometown team Kristianstads IK before moving to the Elitserien with Malmö Redhawks where he spent four seasons . 

He had a brief spell with HV71 in 2002 before returning to Malmö . 

In 2003 , he moved to Brynäs IF and stayed with the team until 2006 when he joined Frölunda HC midway through the 2006 - 07 season . 

In 2007 , he moved in Finland to play in the SM - liiga for Ilves and also had a brief spell with HK Acroni Jesenice of the Erste Bank Eishockey Liga .
