" January February " is a song by Scottish singer Barbara Dickson released on 25 January , 1980 by Epic Records . 

It reached # 11 in the UK Singles Chart in April 1980 , spending 10 weeks in the chart . 

It also made the Top 20 in the German and South African charts . 

The song signalled a new direction in her career , moving into the mainstream pop genre . 

It was written and produced by Alan Tarney who had recently resurrected Cliff Richard 's career with his 1979 no.1 hit " We Do n't Talk Anymore " . 

Mary Roos recorded a German version of the single entitled " Wenn Ich Dich Nicht Halten Kann " ( " If I Can not Hold You " ) .
