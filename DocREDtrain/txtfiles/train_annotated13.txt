Samuel Herbert Cohen ( 26 October 1918 â€“ 7 October 1969 ) was an Australian politician , and was the first Jew to be elected to the Australian Senate . 

He was born in Sydney , he was educated at Wesley College , Melbourne and then the University of Melbourne . 

He was active in left - wing student politics , and as President of the Melbourne University Students ' Representative Council was a founding member of the executive committee of the National Union of Australian University Students . 

He served in the military from 1942 to 1943 and became a barrister in 1946 . 

In 1961 , he was elected to the Australian Senate as a Labor Senator for Victoria . 

He held the seat until his death in 1969 ; Bill Brown was appointed to replace him .
