Infinity at Brickell is a skyscraper in Miami , Florida , United States . 

It is located in Downtown 's Brickell Financial District . 

When it topped out , it was the 7th tallest building in Miami and Florida . 

Although not in the same complex , it was built across the street from Infinity II site , hence its name . 

The location is in southwestern Brickell , on South Miami Avenue near 13th Street . 

The building opened in 2008 . 

It is tall , and has 52 floors . 

It is about shorter than Infinity II was supposed to be . 

The lower floors of the building are dedicated to office space and retail , while the upper floors are used for residential units . 

The architects are Adache Group Architects and Borges & Associates .
