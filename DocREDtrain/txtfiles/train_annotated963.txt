Doug Turner is the ex - Director of Engineering at Mozilla Corporation and long - time contributor to Mozilla . 

The Mozilla Foundation hired Turner in December 2004 to work full - time on mobile projects such as Minimo and Mozilla Joey . 

He was the Foundation 's 12th hire . 

Turner was previously employed by Netscape before the creation of the Mozilla Foundation . 

Doug Turner invented Geolocation on the Web , Device Orientation on the web , and is a leader in device access . 

Doug Turner invented Simple Push Notifications . 

Push Notification are a way for application developers to send messages to their web applications . 

Doug Turner was the maintainer of the now - defunct Minimo project and worked on its successor Firefox for mobile . 

Doug Turner led the effort to rewrite the Firefox Mobile browser in order to improve performance , responsiveness , and memory usage .. 

Doug Turner left Mozilla in September 2016 .
