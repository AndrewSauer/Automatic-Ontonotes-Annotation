The Koala Brothers is an Australian - British - American stop - motion animated children 's television show that features the adventures of two koala brothers , Frank and Buster , as they seek to help their neighbours in a sleepy town in the Australian Outback . 

A notable quality of the series is that although there can be differences of opinion and occasional instances of antisocial or thoughtless behaviour , there are no villains or disagreeable characters . 

Rather , the emphasis is on helping and being a good friend . 

Originally aired on ABC Kids ( Australia ) , the show later aired on CBeebies in the United Kingdom , having premiered on 1 September 2003 and Playhouse Disney in the United States , as well as various other international stations .
