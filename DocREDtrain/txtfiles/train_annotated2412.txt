Claude Pascal ( Paris , February 19 , 1921 – Paris , February 28 , 2017 ) was a French composer . 

After studying at the Conservatoire de Paris , he obtained the 1945 Premier Prix de Rome for the cantata , La farce du contre Bandier . 

After a brief period as conductor of the Opéra - Comique , Pascal became professor at the Paris Conservatoire in 1952 , a position he held until his retirement in 1987 . 

From 1969 to 1979 he worked as a music critic for Le Figaro , and from 1983 to 1991 he was an expert on copyright issues at the Paris Court of Appeals . 

Pascal 's extensive work as a composer includes practically every musical genre . 

The discography of his works consists of more than thirty CDs .
