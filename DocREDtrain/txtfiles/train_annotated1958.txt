It Wo n't Be Soon Before Long is the second studio album by the American pop rock band Maroon 5 . 

It was released in May 2007 by A&M ; Octone Records , as the follow - up to their debut album Songs About Jane ( 2002 ) . 

The name was inspired by a phrase the band adopted to keep themselves motivated while on their tour . 

The album debuted at number one on the U.S. 

Billboard 200 , selling around 430,000 copies in its first week and , as of 2011 , approximately 5 million copies worldwide . 

It is the first feature drummer Matt Flynn . 

The band 's former drummer , percussionist and background vocalist – Ryan Dusick , who officially left the band in 2006 due to serious wrist and shoulder injuries , was credited as the " Musical Director " . 

The band embarked on the It Wo n't Be Soon Before Long Tour ( 2007 – 2008 ) to support of the album .
