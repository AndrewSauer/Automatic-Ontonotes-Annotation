JPEG XR ( JPEG extended range ) is a still - image compression standard and file format for continuous tone photographic images , based on technology originally developed and patented by Microsoft under the name HD Photo ( formerly Windows Media Photo ) . 

It supports both lossy and lossless compression , and is the preferred image format for Ecma-388 Open XML Paper Specification documents . 

Support for the format is available in Adobe Flash Player 11.0 , Adobe AIR 3.0 , Sumatra PDF 2.1 , Windows Imaging Component , .NET Framework 3.0 , Windows Vista , Windows 7 , Windows 8 , Internet Explorer 9 , Internet Explorer 10 , Internet Explorer 11 , Pale Moon 27.2.As of August 2014 , there were still no cameras that shoot photos in the JPEG XR ( .JXR ) format .
