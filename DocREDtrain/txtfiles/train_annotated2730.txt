The Basilica of Saint Mary in the Old Town historic district , of Alexandria , Virginia , ( United States ) and is a minor basilica and parish of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Arlington in Virginia . 

St. Mary 's Basilica is the oldest Roman Catholic church in the Commonwealth of Virginia and located in one of its oldest cities in Old Town Alexandria . 

It was founded in 1795 by the Very Reverend Francis Ignatius Neale , S.J. , first President of Georgetown University , ( then known as Georgetown College ) in present - day western Washington , D.C .. 

On December 6 , 2017 , Pope Francis designated the parish church as a minor basilica . 

The decision was announced on January 15 , 2018 , and the basilica held its inauguration Mass on September 8 , 2018 .
