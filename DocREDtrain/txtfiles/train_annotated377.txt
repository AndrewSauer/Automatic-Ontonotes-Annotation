myki ( ) is a reloadable contactless smartcard ticketing system used on public transport in Victoria , Australia . 

The system is promoted by Public Transport Victoria and is valid on most public transport services in Melbourne and regional Victoria . 

The system was developed by Kamco ( Keane Australia Micropayment Consortium ) . 

The initial 10-year contract was worth approximately A$ 1.5 billion , described by The Age as " the [ world 's ] biggest for a smartcard ticketing system " . 

The myki card is a re - usable , credit card - sized , contactless smartcard that stores value which can be used as payment for public transport fares . 

Ticketing requirements for trains , trams and buses in Melbourne are mainly contained in the Transport ( Ticketing ) Regulations 2006 and the Victorian Fares and Ticketing Manual .
