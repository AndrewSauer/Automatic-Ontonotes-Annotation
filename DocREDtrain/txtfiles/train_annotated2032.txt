The Buchan Caves are a group of limestone caves that include the Royal Cave and the Fairy Cave , located south - west of , in the East Gippsland region of the Australian state of Victoria . 

They have a total length of between , and six entrances . 

The Buchan Cave Reserve has been transferred back to the Gunaikurnai Nation and is jointly managed with the state . 

The limestone rock at Buchan was laid down during the Devonian period about 300   – 400 million years ago . 

At the time , the sea covered this area of East Gippsland which was alive with shellfish and coral . 

Their remains were deposited in layers and over the years compacted to form limestone . 

The caves were formed by solution of the limestone . 

The Buchan Caves are located approximately east northeast ( or five hours ' drive ) from Melbourne , along the Princes Highway , north of Lakes Entrance .
