Głubczyce County ( ) is a unit of territorial administration and local government ( powiat ) in Opole Voivodeship , south - western Poland , on the Czech border . 

It came into being on January 1 , 1999 , as a result of the Polish local government reforms passed in 1998 . 

Its administrative seat and largest town is Głubczyce , which lies south of the regional capital Opole . 

The county also contains the towns of Kietrz , lying south - east of Głubczyce , and Baborów , south - east of Głubczyce .
