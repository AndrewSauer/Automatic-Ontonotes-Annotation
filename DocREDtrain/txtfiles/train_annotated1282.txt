Sulafjorden is a fjord ( more accurately , a sound ) in Sula Municipality in Møre og Romsdal county , Norway . 

It is located on the border of Sula Municipality and Hareid Municipality . 

The great Storfjorden flows out into the Sulafjorden which then flows out into the Breidsundet sound , and on into the ocean . 

The long fjord is about wide and it reaches a maximum depth of below sea level . 

The island of Sula sits on the northeast side of the fjord and the island of Hareidlandet is to the southwest . 

The main settlements along the fjord include the villages of Brandal , Hareid , and Hjørungavåg , all on Hareidlandet . 

The island of Sula has very few inhabitants along the Sulafjorden .
