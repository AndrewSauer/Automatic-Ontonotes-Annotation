Russell Boyd ACS ASC ( born 21 April 1944 ) is an Australian cinematographer . 

He rose to prominence with his highly praised work on Picnic at Hanging Rock ( 1975 ) , the first of several collaborations with director Peter Weir . 

He is also a member of the Australian Cinematographers Society ( ACS ) and the American Society of Cinematographers ( ASC ) . 

Boyd served as cinematographer for Tender Mercies , a 1983 film about an alcoholic country singer played by Robert Duvall . 

Boyd largely utilized available light to give the film a natural feeling which director Bruce Beresford said was crucial to the movie . 

Actress Tess Harper said Boyd was so quiet during filming that he mostly used only three words : " Yeah , right and sure . "
