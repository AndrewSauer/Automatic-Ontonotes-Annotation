" Ike 's Wee Wee " is the fourth episode of the second season of the American animated television series South Park . 

The 18th episode of the series overall , it first aired on Comedy Central in the United States on May 27 , 1998 . 

In the episode , school counselor Mr. Mackey is fired , and turns to drugs . 

Meanwhile , the boys misconstrue what circumcision entails , and try to save Kyle 's younger brother Ike from his upcoming bris . 

The episode was written and directed by series co - creator Trey Parker . 

" Ike 's Wee Wee " satirizes certain attitudes towards drug users , and explores whether family can only mean those who are related by blood . 

This episode introduced Ike 's backstory as an adopted Canadian child . 

" Ike 's Wee Wee " received positive responses from critics , who especially praised the episode for its touching moments .
