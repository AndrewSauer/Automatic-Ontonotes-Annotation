Ejaz Ahmed Chaudhary commonly referred to as Ejaz Chaudhary ( born 15 September 1956 ) is a prominent Pakistani politician , social worker , civil engineer by profession and an ex - deputy mayor of Lahore . 

Chaudhary was previously a member of Jamaat - e - Islami where he spent 35 years of his political career before leaving the party , by 2007 Chaudhary had joined Imran Khan 's Pakistan Tehreek - e - Insaf after receiving a personal invitation from Khan himself to join the party . 

He is currently serving as Imran Khan 's chief political advisor , and served as the Punjab President of the party from 2013 - 15 . 

After becoming Punjab President he serves on both the Central executive committee and Core committee ( Politburo ) Chaudhary 's political views are frequently described as moderate , based on the principals of Islam and of communal tolerance He frequently helps female members of his party by organising protests to condemn the abuse and assault of women in Pakistan A bill against Domestic violence had been sent by the Chaudhary to the Punjab assembly , which after pending a vote for 3 years was approved by the government and opposition Chaudhary is a former deputy mayor of Lahore .
