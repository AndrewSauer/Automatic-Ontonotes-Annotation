Sue Ryder is a charity ( originally called the Sue Ryder Foundation and more recently Sue Ryder Care ) which was founded in 1953 by Sue Ryder , with the creation of a nursing home in Suffolk , UK . 

Sue Ryder supports people with complex needs and life - threatening illnesses across the UK and internationally . 

The charity is headquartered at Upper Woburn Place in London and is a registered charity in England & Wales . 

It dropped the word " Care " from its operating name in April 2011 after a public consultation suggested that it sounded unclear , corporate and distant .
