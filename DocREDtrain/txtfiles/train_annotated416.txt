The Espoo Cathedral ( , ) is a medieval stone church in Espoo , Finland and the seat of the Diocese of Espoo of the Evangelical Lutheran Church of Finland . 

The cathedral is located in the district of Espoon keskus , near the Espoonjoki river . 

The oldest parts of the church were completed in the 1480s and it is thus the oldest preserved building in the city . 

The church became a cathedral in 2004 after the Diocese of Espoo was split off from the Diocese of Helsinki . 

The cathedral grounds include a graveyard , a vicarage and a parish hall completed in 1995 . 

In addition to being the seat of the Diocese of Espoo , it serves as the church for the Espoo Cathedral Parish and hosts various concerts and other events including the " Organ Night and Aria " concert series .
