The Toronto Coach Terminal is the central bus station for inter - city services in Toronto , Ontario , Canada . 

It is located at 610 Bay Street , in the city 's Downtown . 

The terminal is owned by Toronto Coach Terminal Inc. , a wholly owned subsidiary of the Toronto Transit Commission ( TTC ) . 

The TTC managed the station directly until July 8 , 2012 , when it was leased out in its entirety to bus lines Coach Canada and Greyhound Canada for $ 1.2 million annually . 

Opened in 1931 as the Gray Coach Terminal , the Art Deco style terminal was home base for Gray Coach , an interurban bus service then owned by the TTC . 

It replaced an earlier open air terminal , Gray Line Terminal .
