Emil Boc (; born 6 September 1966 ) is a Romanian politician who was Prime Minister of Romania from 22 December 2008 until 6 February 2012 and is the current Mayor of Cluj - Napoca , the largest city of Transylvania , where he was first elected in June 2004 . 

Boc was also the president of the Democratic Liberal Party , which proposed and supported him as Prime Minister in late 2008 , from December 2004 until June 2012 . 

On 13 October 2009 , his cabinet fell after losing a motion of no confidence in Parliament . 

He was acting as the head of acting cabinet until a new Prime Minister and cabinet were confirmed by Parliament .
