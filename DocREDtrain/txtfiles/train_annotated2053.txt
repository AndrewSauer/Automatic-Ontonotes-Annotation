The Tuen Ma line ( ) is a Hong Kong MTR railway line due to open in 2019 . 

Part of the Sha Tin to Central Link project , it will incorporate and join the existing West Rail and Ma On Shan lines via new stations along the Shatin to Central Link ( Hung Hom to Tai Wai section ) . 

The line will have 27 stations , more than any other , and is expected to open in 2019 . 

During planning and construction , this line was codenamed “ East West Corridor ” ( ) until 25 May 2018 , when the finalised name was announced by MTR Corporation to reflect the districts of the termini it serves , namely Tuen Mun and Ma On Shan .
