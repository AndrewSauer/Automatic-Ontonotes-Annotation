Kim Yoon - seok ( born January 21 , 1968 ) is a South Korean actor . 

Kim 's theater background first led him to be cast in minor roles on film and television . 

His breakout role came as the villain in gambling film ( 2006 ) , but it was his performance as an ex - cop turned pimp in surprise hit The Chaser ( 2008 ) that brought him acting awards and stardom in his forties . 

Kim has since become an acclaimed actor in South Korea , with notable performances in films such as Running Turtle ( 2009 ) , The Yellow Sea ( 2010 ) , Punch ( 2011),The Thieves ( 2012 ) , ( 2013 ) , Sea Fog ( 2014 ) , The Classified File ( 2015 ) , The Priests ( 2015 ) and ( 2017 ) .
