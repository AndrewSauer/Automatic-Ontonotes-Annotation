The Panglong Conference ( ) , held in February 1947 , was an historic meeting that took place at Panglong in the Shan States in Burma between the Shan , Kachin and Chin ethnic minority leaders and Aung San , head of the interim Burmese government . 

Aung Zan Wai , Pe Khin , Bo Hmu Aung , Sir Maung Gyi , Dr. Sein Mya Maung and Myoma U Than Kywe were among the negotiators of the historical Panglong Conference negotiated with Bamar representative General Aung San and other ethnic leaders in 1947 . 

All these leaders unanimously decided to join the Union of Burma . 

On the agenda was the united struggle for independence from Britain and the future of Burma after independence as a unified republic .
