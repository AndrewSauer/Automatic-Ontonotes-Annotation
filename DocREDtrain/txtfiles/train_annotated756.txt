Hawarden Airport ( ) , is an airport near Hawarden in Flintshire , Wales , near the border with England and west southwest of the English city of Chester . 

Aviation Park Group ( APG ) is based at the airport and provides handling and related services to private clients . 

APG has a longterm tenancy agreement with Airbus UK , giving sole handling rights at the site . 

A large Airbus factory , which produces aircraft wings , is located at the airport . 

The factory is known as the Broughton factory , named after the nearest village . 

Hawarden Aerodrome has a CAA Ordinary Licence ( Number P786 ) that allows flights for the public transport of passengers or for flying instruction as authorised by the licensee ( Airbus UK Limited ) .
