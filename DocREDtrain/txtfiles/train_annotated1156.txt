The Related Companies , L.P. is an American privately owned real estate firm in New York City , with offices and major developments in Boston , Chicago , Los Angeles , Las Vegas , Miami , San Francisco , Abu Dhabi , London , São Paulo and Shanghai . 

It has more than 3,000 employees . 

Related owns more than 8,000 luxury residential rental units in New York City , more than anyone else . 

Related manages about $ 4 billion of equity capital on behalf of sovereign wealth funds , public pension plans , multi - managers , endowments , Taft Hartley benefits plans , and family offices . 

It has developed mixed - use projects such as Time Warner Center in New York City , as well as CityPlace in West Palm Beach . 

Related is currently developing the Hudson Yards Redevelopment Project , which comprises in Manhattan ’s Chelsea and Hell 's Kitchen neighborhoods .
