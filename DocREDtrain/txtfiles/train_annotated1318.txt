Mullion ( ) is a civil parish and village on the Lizard Peninsula in south Cornwall , England , United Kingdom . 

The nearest town is Helston approximately five miles ( 8   km ) to the north .. 

Mullion civil parish encompasses the church town ( now known simply as Mullion ) and four smaller settlements : Mullion Cove and Predannack to the southwest ; Trewoon and Meaver to the east . 

Mullion is bordered by the parishes of Gunwalloe and Cury to the north , Grade - Ruan to the east , Landewednack to the south , and by Mount 's Bay to the west . 

The parish also includes Mullion Island , a uninhabited island approximately half a mile ( 0.8   km ) offshore from Mullion Cove . 

The island is home to large colonies of seabirds and is owned by the National Trust .
