Franklin County Regional Airport , formerly known as Chambersburg Municipal Airport , is a general aviation airport located three miles ( 5 Â  km ) north of the Borough of Chambersburg , in Franklin County , Pennsylvania , USA . 

The airport is situated approximately 45 miles southwest of Harrisburg . 

In 2004 , the Susquehanna Area Regional Airport Authority ( SARAA ) bought Franklin County Regional Airport and formed a Study Advisory Group ( SAG ) to develop a list of recommended improvements at the airport . 

Those improvements included : rehabilitation of the runway , taxiway , ramp area and other landside faculties , additional parking , signage , hangars , an automated weather observation station ( AWOS ) and a fueling service . 

The improvements will be made on a priority basis and as grant monies become available .
