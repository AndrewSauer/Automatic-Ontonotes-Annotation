Born in Dallas , Texas , Jones received her bachelor 's degree from Emerson College and her law degree from Boston College Law School . 

In 1993 , President Bill Clinton appointed Jones as United States Attorney for the Southern District of Texas . 

She was the first African - American woman to serve in that position . 

Before her appointment as U.S. Attorney , Jones was also the first African - American woman to serve on the First Court of the Texas Courts of Appeals . 

In later years , she worked as litigation counsel for Hewlett - Packard Company and was an adjunct professor at the University of Houston Law School .
