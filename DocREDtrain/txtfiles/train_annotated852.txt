Jasun Martz is an American record producer , composer , musician , fine artist , creative director and sculptor who has worked on several # 1 internationally best selling hit records but is probably best known for his contemporary classical symphonies . 

He has recorded with Michael Jackson , toured with Frank Zappa and helped arrange one of rock music 's best selling hits : " We Built this City " by Starship . 

Also a renowned New York based painter and sculptor , Martz has lived in New York , Los Angeles and London and has created and exhibited " raw expressionist " paintings and papier - mâché sculpture inspired by the subway passengers he encounters in each city . 

He collaborated with French modern master Jean Dubuffet ( the founder of art brut ) on Martz 's critically acclaimed avant - garde/ contemporary classical symphony entitled The Pillory .
