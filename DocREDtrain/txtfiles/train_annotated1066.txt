Galena is the largest city in and the county seat of Jo Daviess County , Illinois , with a population of 3,429 at the 2010 census . 

A section of the city is listed on the National Register of Historic Places as the Galena Historic District . 

The city is named for the mineral galena , which was mined by Native Americans in the area for over a thousand years . 

Owing to these deposits , Galena was the site of the first major mineral rush in the United States . 

By 1828 , the population was estimated at 10,000 , rivaling the population of Chicago at the time . 

The city emerged as the largest steamboat hub on the Mississippi River north of St. Louis , Missouri . 

Galena was the home of Ulysses S. Grant and eight other Civil War generals . 

Today , the city is a tourist destination known for its history , architecture , and resorts .
