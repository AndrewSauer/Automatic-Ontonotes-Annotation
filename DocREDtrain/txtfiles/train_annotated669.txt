Feldwebel ( Fw or F ) , literally " field usher " , is a non - commissioned officer ( NCO ) rank in several countries . 

The rank originated in Germany , and is also used in Switzerland , Finland , Sweden , and Estonia . 

The rank has also been used in Russia , Austria - Hungary , and Bulgaria .
