Jasenko Đorđrević is an artist from Tuzla , Bosnia and Herzegovina who was born on June 9 , 1983 . 

Jasenko is one of the few people in the world who specialize in making sculptures on the tip of a pencil ( the graphite part ) . 

He goes by a pseudonym TOLDart . 

He has made about 200 sculptures and still continues to make more . 

Jasenko still lives in Tuzla working as an aikido instructor . 

Because of some connections that he has made with people in different countries he travels around Europe on his exhibitions . 

He has been making sculptures for a long time , but quite recently he became popular through social media and since then he has been invited to Norway , Greece , Germany and many other places .
