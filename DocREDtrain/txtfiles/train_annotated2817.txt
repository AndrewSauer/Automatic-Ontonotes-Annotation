The Oregon State Police ( OSP ) is a law enforcement agency of the U.S. state of Oregon . 

The OSP is charged to enforce all of Oregon 's criminal laws , including assisting local law enforcement agencies . 

Colonel Travis Hampton has served as Superintendent since July 1 , 2016 . 

The agency differs from other state police , such as the California Highway Patrol , in that their primary focus is not limited to highway and traffic enforcement . 

The OSP is one of the few law enforcement agencies in the United States that monitors the security of the state lottery . 

Oregon State Police has primary jurisdiction on state highways and all other state owned property , and it often responds to incidents in rural areas when other local agencies are unable to respond or need extra assistance .
