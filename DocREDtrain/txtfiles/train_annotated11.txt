Monson is a town in Piscataquis County , Maine , United States . 

The population was 686 at the 2010 census . 

The town is located on Route 15 which is a somewhat major route north to the well known Moosehead Lake Region , to which Monson is sometimes considered a gateway . 

This route eventually leads to the Canadian Province of Quebec . 

The town is well known in local and state history , but is minimally known elsewhere , although contributions by the town , and its location are notable . 

It is the last town located on the Appalachian Trail at the beginning ( or end ) of the 100-Mile Wilderness . 

It is not uncommon to see many hikers in town resting before ( or after ) their trip into the Wilderness , and several lodging places in town cater directly to these hikers .
