Christina Schmuck ( sometimes listed as Christa Schmuck , born 26 January 1944 ) is a West German luger who competed during the late 1960s and early 1970s . 

At the 1968 Winter Olympics in Grenoble , she originally finished fifth in the women 's singles event , but was awarded the silver medal upon the disqualifications of the East German team of Ortrun Enderlein ( who finished first ) , Anna - Maria Müller ( second ) , and Angela Knösel ( fourth ) when the East Germans were discovered to have their runners being illegally heated . 

Schmuck also won two medals in the women 's singles event at the FIL World Luge Championships with a silver in 1970 and a bronze in 1969 . 

Both championships took place in Königssee , West Germany . 

She also won two medals the women 's singles event at the FIL European Luge Championships with a gold in 1967 and a bronze in 1970 .
