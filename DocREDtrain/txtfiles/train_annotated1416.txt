Richard Jasek ( born 1964/1965 ) is a Czechoslovakia - born Australian television producer , writer and director . 

Jasek was born and raised Prague until his family fled the communist regime and settled in Brisbane . 

Jasek chose to become a filmmaker after he discovered his father 's camera . 

He would later enrol at the Australian Film , Television and Radio School . 

Jasek made a short production for SBS Television and travelled to the United Kingdom to work for The National Film School as a guest lecturer . 

Upon his return to Australia , Jasek began working in television and he is best known for his work with dramas A Country Practice , City Homicide and McLeod 's Daughters . 

In October 2011 , it was announced that Jasek had taken over the role of executive producer of Neighbours .
