He was awarded the medal for rescuing his crewmates during the sinking of his ship , the , in the Mississippi River . 

Under heavy fire from a Confederate artillery battery , Corcoran and three other men swam back and forth between the riverbank and the sinking ship , helping crewmen who could not swim reach shore . 

The four men then towed a small boat , carrying wounded sailors and the ship 's commander , to the safety of Union forces .
