Elżbieta Zawacka (; 19 March 1909 – 10 January 2009 ) , known also by her war - time nom de guerre Zo , was a Polish university professor , scouting instructor , SOE agent and a freedom fighter during World War II . 

She was also a Brigadier General of the Polish Army ( the second and last woman in the history of the Polish Army to hold this rank ) , promoted by President Lech Kaczyński on 3 May 2006 . 

The only woman among the Cichociemni , she served as a courier for the Home Army , carrying letters and other documents from Nazi - occupied Poland to the Polish government in exile and back . 

Her regular route ran from Warsaw through Berlin and Sweden to London . 

She was also responsible for organizing routes for other couriers of the Home Army .
