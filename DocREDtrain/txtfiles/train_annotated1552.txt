You Do Your Thing is the fourth studio album by American country music duo Montgomery Gentry . 

It was released in 2004 ( see 2004 in country music ) and has been certified platinum by the RIAA . 

The album produced the duo 's first Number One hit on the Billboard country music charts in " If You Ever Stop Loving Me " ; other singles included the title track , " Gone " , and " Something to Be Proud Of " ( also a Number One ) . 

" If It 's the Last Thing I Do " was also recorded by Brooks & Dunn on their 2001 album , Steers & Stripes , and in 2004 by James Otto on his album Days of Our Lives . 

Both of these renditions were titled " The Last Thing I Do " .
