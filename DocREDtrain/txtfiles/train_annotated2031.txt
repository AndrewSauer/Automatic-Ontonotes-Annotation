James Manuel Costa , ComM ( born April 13 , 1952 ) is the U.S. Representative for , serving as a Democrat in Congress since his initial election in 2004 . 

He previously served in the California State Assembly from 1978 until 1994 , and in the State Senate from 1994 until 2002 . 

At the time of his election to the California State Legislature , he was the youngest member of the legislature at the age of 26 . 

During his time in the California State Assembly , he served as the Majority Caucus Chair . 

California 's 16th congressional district takes in large and predominantly Latino portions of Fresno . 

Costa is chair of the Blue Dog Coalition and ranking member of the United States House Agriculture Subcommittee on Rural Development , Research , Biotechnology , and Foreign Agriculture .
