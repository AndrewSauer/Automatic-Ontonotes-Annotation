Yahya Skaf , also spelled Yehia Skaff ( Arabic : يحيى سكاف ) , from the Bhanine ( Menyeh ) district of Lebanon , is a person claimed to have been arrested by Israel on 11 March 1978 for participation in the Coastal Road massacre . 

Israel claims he was killed during battle with his body never found . 

It is claimed by the Khiam Center that his family and some released detainees have borne witness to his imprisonment . 

A supporter of the Syrian Social Nationalist Party , he subsequently joined Fatah to participate in military operations against Israel . 

Hezbollah leader Hassan Nasrallah suggested Skaf be a part of a prisoner exchange during the 2006 Lebanon War . 

However , he was not included in the July 2008 prisoner exchange .
