USS Haas ( DE-424 ) was a John C. Butler - class destroyer escort acquired by the U.S. Navy during World War II . 

The primary purpose of the destroyer escort was to escort and protect ships in convoy , in addition to other tasks as assigned , such as patrol or radar picket . 

Haas ( DE-424 ) was named in honor of John William Haas who was awarded the Navy Cross after he was lost in action 4 June 1942 during the Battle of Midway . 

She was launched 20 March 1944 by the Brown Shipbuilding Co. , Houston , Texas ; sponsored by Mrs. Gladys Winifred Haas , widow of Chief Machinist Haas : and commissioned 2 August 1944 , Lt . Comdr . 

A. M. White , USNR , in command .
