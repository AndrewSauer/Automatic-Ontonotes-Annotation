Prem Bandhan (; translation : bond of love ) is a 1979 Indian Hindi film directed by Ramanand Sagar . 

The film stars Rajesh Khanna , Rekha and Moushumi Chatterjee as the lead actors and is supported by A. K. Hangal , Prema Narayan , Bhagwan , Keshto Mukherjee and Lalita Pawar . 

The film 's music is by Laxmikant Pyarelal . 

This film , along with Amar Deep , marked the comeback of Rajesh Khanna , as these were his commercially successful films after a period of one full year as in the year 1978 , none of his films became huge hits at the box office , with the exception of Bhola Bhala . 

The news magazine " The Indian " noted that " Rajesh Khanna makes a sincere attempt to recover his popularity as " superstar " and succeeds . 

He gives natural touching performance " . 

The story revolves around the adventures of Rajesh Khanna when he loses his memory by meeting in an accident , falling prey to amnesia and falls in love with a fisher woman and gets married , whereas his sophisticated city bred girlfriend keeps waiting for him in the city .
