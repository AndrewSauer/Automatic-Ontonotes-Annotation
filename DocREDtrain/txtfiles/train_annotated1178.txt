The Winnipeg Blue Bombers ( officially the Winnipeg Football Club ) are a Canadian football team based in Winnipeg , Manitoba . 

They are currently members of the West Division of the Canadian Football League ( CFL ) . 

They play their home games at Investors Group Field after many years of playing at the since demolished Canad Inns Stadium . 

The Blue Bombers were founded in 1930 as the Winnipeg Football Club , which remains the organization 's legal name today . 

Since that time , they have won the league 's Grey Cup championship 10 times , most recently in 1990 . 

With 10 wins , they have the third - highest win total in the Grey Cup although they are currently the team with the longest Grey Cup drought . 

The Blue Bombers were the first team not located in Ontario or Quebec to win a championship and hold the record for most Grey Cup appearances with 25 .
