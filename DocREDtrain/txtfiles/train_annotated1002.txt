New Zealand and Canada have a longstanding relationship that has been fostered by both countries ' shared history and culture , by their membership of the Commonwealth of Nations and links between residents of both countries . 

The two countries have a common Head of State in Queen Elizabeth II ( legally , the Queen is equally and separately the Head of State of both nations , as Queen of Canada and Queen of New Zealand ) . 

New Zealand and Canada also have links through business or trade relations , the United Nations , the Commonwealth of Nations and mutual treaty agreements . 

New Zealand - Canada relations are therefore important to both countries .
