Lee Shying - jow or Lee Hsiang - chou ( ) is a military personnel of the Republic of China ( Taiwan ) . 

He is the incumbent Minister of the Veterans Affairs Council , and was the 4th Commanding General of the Republic of China Army ( ROCA ) , 8th Deputy Minister of National Defense ( MND ) and the 15th Director - General of the National Security Bureau ( NSB ) . 

General Lee Hsiang - chou is sincere and open to criticism , and time has proven that his achievements are prominent . 

During his years of service , he focused on the value of royalty , and paid close attentions to training and readiness . 

General Lee was also called as the " best Army Commanding General within these 10 years " among the ROC Army itself and the Taiwanese civic and netizens .
