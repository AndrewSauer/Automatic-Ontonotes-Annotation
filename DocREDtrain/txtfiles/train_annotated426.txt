Midway High School is a public high school located in the city of Waco , Texas , USA and classified as a 6A school by the UIL . 

It is part of the Midway Independent School District , which serves the Waco , Texas area . 

Although the school is located in southwestern Waco , most students live in the suburbs of Woodway and Hewitt . 

The school was formerly located in Hewitt until the fall of 2003 , when the current high school was built a half - mile away . 

The previous building is now Midway Middle School .
