" Desperate Souls " is the eighth episode of the first season of the American Fairy Tale / Drama television series Once Upon a Time . 

It was written by consulting producer Jane Espenson , and was directed by Michael Waxman . 

Espenson wrote it to give viewers some " crucial facts " about Rumpelstiltskin 's origins . 

Guest stars included Brad Dourif , Giancarlo Esposito , Beverley Elliott , Ty Olsson , Meghan Ory , and Dylan Schmid . 

The series takes place in the fictional seaside town of Storybrooke , Maine , in which the residents are actually characters from various fairy tales that were transported to the " real world " town by a powerful curse . 

In this episode , Emma Swan ( Jennifer Morrison ) runs for Sheriff against Regina 's ( Lana Parrilla ) candidate , Sidney Glass ( Esposito ) . 

Meanwhile , in the episode 's flashbacks , Rumpelstiltskin 's ( Robert Carlyle ) backstory is revealed . 

" Desperate Souls " first aired in the United States on ABC on January 8 , 2012 . 

Its broadcast was watched by an estimated 10.35 million viewers , a 29 percent increase from the previous episode . 

Television critics gave mostly positive reviews to the episode ; most praised Carlyle 's performance , but one noted that the storytelling was beginning to " feel stale . "
