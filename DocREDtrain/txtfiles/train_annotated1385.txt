Florian Skilang Temengil ( born November 4 , 1986 ) is a Palauan wrestler at Newman University in Wichita , Kansas . 

Skilang competed in the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing , China in the freestyle wrestling . 

He lost his bout to Ottó Aubéli of Hungary 0 - 6 , 1 - 7 . 

However , he managed to score the first ever point for Palauan Olympic wrestlers , since his countryman John Tarkong Jr. had failed to score any points four years earlier , as would his countryman Elgin Loren Elwais in the Greco - Roman -55   kg category . 

He competed for Palau at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro in the 125 kg division . 

He was defeated by Daniel Ligeti of Hungary in the first round . 

He was the flag bearer for Palau during the Parade of Nations .
