Igor Yuriyevich Nikulin (; born August 14 , 1960 in Moscow , Russian SFSR ) is a former hammer thrower who represented the USSR , the Unified Team , and later Russia . 

He won the bronze medal at the 1992 Olympic Games in Barcelona , Spain .
