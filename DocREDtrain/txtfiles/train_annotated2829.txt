Muhlenbergia glomerata is a species of grass known by the common names spiked muhly and marsh muhly . 

It is native to North America , where it occurs across Canada and the northern half of the United States . 

This grass produces branching stems up to 1.2 meters tall from a network of rhizomes . 

The inflorescence is a narrow panicle of spikelets which are up to 8 millimeters long . 

This grass is found in moist areas in various habitat types . 

It grows in bogs , marshes , meadows , ditches , fens , swamps , riversides and lakeshores , hot springs , wet forests , alvars , and seasonally flooded land . 

It occurs in cooler , more moist places than many other C species . 

It occurs less often in dry places .
