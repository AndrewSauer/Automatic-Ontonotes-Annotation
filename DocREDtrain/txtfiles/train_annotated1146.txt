" Do Shapeshifters Dream of Electric Sheep ? " 

is the fourth episode of the third season of the American science fiction drama television series Fringe , and the 47th episode overall . 

The first half of the third season alternated entire episodes between the parallel universe ( the " Other Side " ) and the prime universe ( " Our Side " ) .
