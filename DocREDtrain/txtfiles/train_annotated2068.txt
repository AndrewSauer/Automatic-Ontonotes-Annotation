Daniel Ellison ( February 14 , 1886 – August 20 , 1960 ) was a U.S. Representative from Maryland . 

Born in Russian Empire , Ellison was brought to the United States by his parents as an infant . 

He attended the public schools of Baltimore , Maryland , and graduated from Johns Hopkins University in 1907 and from the University of Maryland School of Law of Baltimore in 1909 . 

He was admitted to the bar the same year and commenced practice in Baltimore . 

He was also a longtime member of the Baltimore city council , serving from 1923 to 1942 . 

Ellison was elected as a Republican to the Seventy - eighth Congress ( January 3 , 1943 – January 3 , 1945 ) , but was an unsuccessful candidate for reelection in 1944 to the Seventy - ninth Congress . 

He resumed the practice of law in Baltimore , and also served as member of the Maryland Senate from 1946 to 1950 . 

He died in Baltimore , and is interred in Hebrew Friendship Cemetery .
