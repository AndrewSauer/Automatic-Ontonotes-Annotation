The Ibergeregg Pass is a mountain pass at an elevation in the Swiss canton of Schwyz . 

The pass connects the town of Schwyz and village of Oberiberg , with onward links to Einsiedeln and the Sihlsee . 

The headwaters of the Minster , a tributary of the Sihl , are nearby , and the pass is flanked by the Alpine peaks of Furggelenstock and Firstspitz . 

A mule track has crossed the pass since the middle ages , and the current paved road was constructed in 1873 . 

The pass road has a maximum grade of 14 percent . 

At the summit of the pass are an inn , a chapel , and a ski station . 

The area is a summer and winter resort , with skiing and hiking .
