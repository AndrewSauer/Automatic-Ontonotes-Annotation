PJSC Rosneft Oil Company ( stylized as ROSNEFT ) is a Russian integrated energy company headquartered in the Russian capital of Moscow . 

It is specializing on exploration , extraction , production , refinement , transport , and sale of petroleum , natural gas , and petroleum products . 

The company is controlled by the Russian government through the holding company . 

Its name is a portmanteau of the Russian words Rossiyskaya neft ' ( Russian : Российская нефть - Russian oil ) . 

Rosneft was founded in 1993 as a state enterprise and later incorporated in 1995 , acquiring a number of state - controlled gas and oil assets . 

Rosneft became Russia 's leading oil company after purchasing assets of former oil company Yukos at state - run auctions . 

After acquiring OJSC TNK - BP in 2013 , then - one of the largest oil companies of Russia , Rosneft became the world 's largest publicly - traded petroleum company . 

Rosneft is the third largest company of Russia and the second - largest state - controlled company after Gazprom in Russia in terms of revenue ( ₽ 4,134 billion ) . 

Internationally , it is one of the largest oil companies , ranking 24 in terms of revenue . 

The company operates in more than twenty countries around the world .
