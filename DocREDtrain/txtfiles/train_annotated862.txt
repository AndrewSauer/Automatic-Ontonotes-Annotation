S. Ali Raza ( 9 January 1925 â€“ 1 November 2007 ) was an Indian film screenwriter and director associated with writing the script for hit films such as Aan , Andaz , Mother India , Reshma Aur Shera , Raja Jani and Dus Numbri . 

In 1968 he won the Filmfare award as the best dialogue writer for Saraswatichandra . 

In 1975 he directed a film titled Pran Jaye Par Vachan Na Jaye . 

He was married to actress Nimmi who appeared in Uran Khatola , Barsaat , Deedar , Amar , Aan , Char Paise , Rajdhani , Shamma , Pooja Ke Phool , Mere Mehboob for which he had written the dialogues . 

He and Nimmi lived in Mumbai until his death at the age of 85 on 1 November 2007 .
