Northern Burlington County Regional High School is a four - year comprehensive public high school that serves students in ninth through twelfth grades from four communities in Burlington County , New Jersey , United States , as part of the Northern Burlington County Regional School District . 

Students are served from Chesterfield Township , Mansfield Township , North Hanover Township and Springfield Township , along with children of USAF personnel based at McGuire Air Force Base . 

First opening to students upon the completion of the current building in 1960 , the school is located in the Columbus section of Mansfield Township . 

The school has been accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Secondary Schools since 1966 .
