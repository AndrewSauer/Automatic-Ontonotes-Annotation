Michael Wilson Ivie ( born August 8 , 1952 ) , is an American former professional baseball player . 

He played as a first baseman in Major League Baseball for the San Diego Padres , San Francisco Giants , Houston Astros , and Detroit Tigers during his career from 1971 to 1983 . 

In addition to playing first base , Ivie put in considerable time as a third baseman , a left fielder and was a designated hitter . 

Ivie is one of only five Major League Baseball players to hit two pinch - hit grand slams in the same season . 

The others are Davey Johnson of the Philadelphia Phillies , Darryl Strawberry of the New York Yankees , Ben Broussard of the Cleveland Indians , and Brooks Conrad of the Atlanta Braves .
