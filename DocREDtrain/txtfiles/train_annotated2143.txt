Packer Park is a neighborhood in the South Philadelphia section of Philadelphia , Pennsylvania , United States that originally included 1,000 homes built in two unique builder developments of Packer Park 1950s and Brinton Estates 1990s . 

It is now one of four residential communities to be designated as Packer Park . 

The original footprint community is also one of four adjacent communities that form Philadelphia 's Sports Complex Special Services District . 

The approximate boundaries are Packer Avenue to the north , Hartranft Street to the south including FDR Park farther south , Broad Street to the east known as the Southern Parkway and I-76 to the far west . 

Packer Park has been considered home to one of the most organized community groups in the South Philadelphia region .
