Isidore Mvouba ( born 1954 ) is a Congolese politician who was Prime Minister of Congo - Brazzaville from 2005 to 2009 . 

He is a member of the Congolese Labour Party ( PCT , or Parti congolais du travail ) and held key positions under President Denis Sassou Nguesso beginning in 1997 . 

Mvouba was Director of the Cabinet of the Head of State from 1997 to 1999 , Minister of Transport from 1999 to 2005 ( with responsibility for coordinating government action beginning in 2002 ) , and Prime Minister from January 2005 to September 2009 . 

After his post of Prime Minister was eliminated , he instead served as Minister of State for Transport , responsible for coordinating ministries relating to infrastructure , from 2009 to 2012 . 

Subsequently he was Minister of State for Industrial Development from 2012 to 2016 . 

He has been President of the National Assembly since 2017 .
