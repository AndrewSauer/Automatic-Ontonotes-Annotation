Slivo Pole ( , ) is a town in northeastern Bulgaria , part of Rousse Province . 

It is the administrative centre of the homonymous Slivo Pole Municipality , which lies in the northeastern part of the Province . 

The town is located five kilometres from the Danube , along the main road from Rousse to Silistra . 

As of December 2009 , it has a population of 3,169 inhabitants . 

Slivo Pole was proclaimed a town on 29 November 2002 .
