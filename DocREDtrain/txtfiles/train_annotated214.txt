Idylwood is a census - designated place ( CDP ) in Fairfax County , Virginia , United States . 

The population was 17,288 at the 2010 census . 

It originally developed as a suburban community along the route of the Washington and Old Dominion Railroad , and later along Virginia State Route 7 . 

The construction of the Capital Beltway in the 1960s , and I-66 and the Orange Line of the Washington Metrorail system in the 1980s , as well as the concurrent development of nearby Tysons Corner into Washington 's leading suburban business district , led to the development of several apartment , townhouse , and small - lot single - family housing complexes , as well as the high - rise Idylwood Towers condominium , in the portion of Idylwood lying to the north of I-66 . 

The area to the south of I-66 remains primarily large - lot single - family .
