Kate Fleetwood ( born 24 September 1972 ) is an English actress . 

She was nominated for a Tony Award for her performance as Lady Macbeth in Chichester Festival Theatre 's Macbeth , which transferred to the West End and Broadway . 

She was nominated for an Olivier Award for her performance as Julie in London Road at the National Theatre . 

She is patron of En Masse Theatre , and joint patron of Escape Insight Arts , Stratford - upon - Avon with her husband Rupert . 

Fleetwood was raised near Stratford - upon - Avon and is a graduate of Exeter University . 

She is married to Rupert Goold with whom she has one son and one daughter . 

She began her career at the Royal Shakespeare Company during her childhood .
