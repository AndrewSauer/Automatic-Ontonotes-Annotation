Eric " Butters " Stough ( born July 31 , 1972 ) is an American animator and producer . 

He is best known as the animation director and a producer on the television series South Park . 

Born in Evergreen , Colorado , Stough attended the University of Colorado at Boulder and graduated in 1995 with a degree in film . 

A longtime friend of Trey Parker , he has worked with him and Matt Stone on most of their projects , including South Park , , Orgazmo , and the Broadway musical The Book of Mormon . 

Stough has won five Primetime Emmy Awards for his work on South Park , as well as a Peabody Award . 

He was the inspiration for the character of Butters Stotch on South Park .
