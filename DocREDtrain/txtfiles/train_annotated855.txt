Unlike most other schools in Helsinki , the school is not owned by the city of Helsinki , but is instead part of the University of Helsinki , which itself is owned directly by the state of Finland . 

The school is considered one of the most prestigious . 

It is the second hardest to get into , in the entire country , tied with Ressun Lukio . 

The most difficult one is Helsingin yliopiston Viikin normaalikoulu , which is also owned by the University of Helsinki . 

The established nickname for the school is Norssi , which also means a student of the school . 

Former students who have graduated from the school are called vanha Norssi ( Finnish for " old Norssi " ) . 

The school has a constant , good - natured rivalry with Ressun lukio ( Ressu Upper Secondary School , previously known as " Helsingin reaalilyseo " ) and Helsingin Suomalainen Yhteiskoulu which are considered even more prestigious than the Normal Lyceum by some .
