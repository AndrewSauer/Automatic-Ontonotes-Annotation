François Missoffe ( 13 October 1919 in Toulon , France – 28 August 2003 in Rouen ) was a French politician and diplomat . 

He was Minister of Youth Affairs and Sport ( " Ministre de la Jeunesse et des Sports " ) in the Government of France between 8 January 1966 and 30 May 1968 . 

He played a minor role in the run - up to events of May 1968 in France . 

On 8 January 1968 , Missoffe was forced by students at the Paris West University Nanterre La Défense to abandon the inauguration of a campus swimming pool . 

He was interrupted while making his speech at the occasion by student leader , Daniel Cohn - Bendit in order to demand free access to the girls ' dormitory . 

Missoffe was Ambassador to Japan from 1964 to 1966 .
