I Am is the fifth studio album by the heavy metal band Becoming the Archetype . 

The album was recorded between May 21 and June 17 and was released on September 18 , 2012 through Solid State Records . 

This album is a departure from the previous effort , as Seth Hecox says , " gone are the sitars and horns of Celestial Completion .

Guitarist / vocalist Seth Hecox is the only original member from the debut album featured on I Am . 

The first single to promote the album was the song " The Time Bender " , released on August 28 , 2012 , as well a lyric video and an official video .
