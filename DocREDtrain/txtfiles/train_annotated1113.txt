Football is the most popular sport in Iceland . 

Iceland hosted the U-18 European Championship in 1997 , but an Icelandic national team has qualified for the final competition of a major tournament only four times — twice by the women 's national team at UEFA Women 's Euro in 2009 and 2013 , and twice by the men 's team at UEFA Euro 2016 and the 2018 FIFA World Cup . 

The only Iceland teams to advance past the group stage at a major tournament are the women in 2013 and the men in 2016 . 

Iceland 's most famous footballer is Eiður Guðjohnsen . 

He has won two Premier League titles for Chelsea F.C. 

as well as the La Liga , Copa del rey and the Champions League title for FC Barcelona . 

There are 20,000 players ( men and women ) registered at clubs .
