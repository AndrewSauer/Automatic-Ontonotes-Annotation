Straits Quay is a shopping mall - cum - marina in George Town , Penang , Malaysia . 

It is located at Seri Tanjung Pinang , a newly developed township that sits on reclaimed land within the George Town suburb of Tanjung Tokong . 

Opened in 2010 , the seaside mall also consists of a marina designed for small recreational vessels , as well as a convention centre . 

Royal Selangor , a Malaysian pewter manufacturer , maintains an outlet within the mall . 

There is also a Performing Arts Centre within the mall . 

In addition , Straits Quay includes luxurious serviced apartments called The Suites @ Straits Quay , which are managed by the Eastern & Oriental Hotel , a colonial seaside hotel located within the heart of George Town .
