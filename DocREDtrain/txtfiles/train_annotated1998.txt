Thank You , Jeeves is a Jeeves comic novel by P.G. 

Wodehouse , first published in the United Kingdom on 16 March 1934 by Herbert Jenkins , London , and in the United States on 23 April 1934 by Little , Brown and Company , New York . 

The story had previously been serialised , in the Strand Magazine in the UK from August 1933 to February 1934 , and in the US in Cosmopolitan Magazine from January to June 1934 . 

Thank You , Jeeves is the first full - length novel in the series of stories following narrator Bertie Wooster and his valet Jeeves , though Jeeves leaves Bertie 's employment for most of this story . 

The novel largely takes place around Chuffnell Hall , the home of Bertie 's friend Lord " Chuffy " Chuffnell , who hopes to sell the house to the wealthy J. Washburn Stoker and is in love with Stoker 's daughter Pauline .
