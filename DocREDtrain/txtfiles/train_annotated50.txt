Mosè in Egitto ( Moses in Egypt ) ( ) is a three - act opera written by Gioachino Rossini to an Italian libretto by Andrea Leone Tottola , which was based on a 1760 play by Francesco Ringhieri , L'Osiride . 

It premièred on 5 March 1818 at the recently reconstructed Teatro San Carlo in Naples , Italy . 

In 1827 Rossini revised the work with a new title : Moïse et Pharaon , ou Le passage de la Mer Rouge ( Moses and Pharaoh , or The Crossing of the Red Sea ) ( ) . 

It was set to a four - act libretto written in French by Luigi Balocchi and Victor - Joseph Étienne de Jouy and the première was given by the Paris Opera at the Salle Le Peletier on 26 March the same year .
