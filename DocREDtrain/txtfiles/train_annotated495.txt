For her son 's funeral in Chicago , Mamie Till insisted that the casket containing his body be left open , because , in her words , " I wanted the world to see what they did to my baby . " 

Born in Mississippi , Till - Mobley moved with her parents to the Chicago area during the Great Migration . 

After her son 's murder she became an educator and activist in the Civil Rights Movement .
