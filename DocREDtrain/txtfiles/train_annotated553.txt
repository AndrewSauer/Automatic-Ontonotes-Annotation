" Test for Echo " is the title track and first single from Canadian rock band Rush 's 16th studio album released in 1996 . 

The song 's lyrics were written by Neil Peart and Pye Dubois with music written by Geddy Lee and Alex Lifeson . 

The song reached # 1 on the Billboard Hot Mainstream Rock Tracks chart . 

The song also peaked at # 6 on the Canadian Singles Chart , which is the band 's highest position on that chart since New World Man peaked at # 1 in 1982 . 

It was the band 's fourth consecutive album to yield a chart - topper on this chart , along with " Stick It Out " from Counterparts , " Show Do n't Tell " from Presto and " Dreamline " from Roll the Bones .
