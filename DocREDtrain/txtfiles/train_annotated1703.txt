Aung Zan Wai (; 19 November 1893 â€“ 29 December 1984 ) was a Burmese politician . 

He was born in Sittwe , Kyauktaw , British Burma in 1893 . 

Aung Zan Wai , along with Pe Khin , Bo Hmu Aung , Sir Maung Gyi , Sein Mya Maung , Myoma U Than Kywe were most important negotiators and leaders of the historical Panglong Conference negotiated with Burma national top leader Aung San and other top leaders in 1947 . 

All these leaders decided to join together to form the Union of Burma . 

The signing is now celebrated as a national holiday , Union Day , in Myanmar . 

He died on 29 December 1984 in Rangoon and left his second wife , San Thar Nu , three daughters and a son .
