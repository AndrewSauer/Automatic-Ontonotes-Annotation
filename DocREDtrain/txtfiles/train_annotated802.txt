Suddenly is the debut album by Korean duo male group J - Walk . 

Former Sechskies members , Kim Jae Duc and Jang Su Won , return as a project group with the name J - Walk . 

Their comeback / debut album has been diligently prepared since their breakup , May 2000 . 

The featured song " Suddenly " contains crossover jazz , R&B - ish ; songs with a pop / ballad format . 

Suddenly was released on 21 March . 

According to Recording Industry Association of Korea , as of the end of December 2002 , the album has sold a cumulative total of 119,317 copies . This album achieved great success so they won SBS Gayo Daejeon Popularity Award with former Sechs Kies member Kang Sunghun in 2002 .
