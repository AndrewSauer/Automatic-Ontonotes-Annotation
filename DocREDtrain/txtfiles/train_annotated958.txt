Morris Sadek ( born December 12 , 1942 ) is a Coptic American lawyer and activist who reportedly brought YouTube video Innocence of Muslims to the attention of the Arabic - speaking world , where it was denounced by protesters at American embassies and consulates . 

His Egyptian citizenship had previously been revoked for promoting the invasion of Egypt and attacking Islam . 

A vocal opponent of Islam , he reportedly protested the Ground Zero Mosque on September 11 , 2010 , with crucifix , Bible , and American flag , stating that " Islam is a religion . " 

On September 6 , 2012 , he is reported to have sent out mails to journalists containing the link to the 14-minute version of the film Innocence of Muslims .
