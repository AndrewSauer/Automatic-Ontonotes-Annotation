The Waggoner Ranch is a historic north Texas ranch located 13 miles south of Vernon , Texas . 

The land was used primarily to raise crops , beef cattle and horses as well as for oil production . 

It was notable for being the largest ranch under one fence in the United States . 

It was originally established in 1852 near Vernon , Texas , by Daniel Waggoner under the name of Dan Waggoner & Son ; his son being William Thomas Waggoner . 

It was acquired by Stan Kroenke , who is married to Ann Walton ( niece of Sam Walton ) , in February 2016 .
