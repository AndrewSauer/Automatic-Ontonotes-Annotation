The Ship That Died of Shame , released in the United States as PT Raiders , is a black - and - white 1955 Ealing Studios crime film directed by Basil Dearden and starring George Baker , Richard Attenborough and Bill Owen . 

The film is based on a story written by Nicholas Monsarrat ( better known as the author of The Cruel Sea ) , which originally appeared in Lilliput magazine in 1952 . 

It was later published in a collection of short stories , The Ship That Died of Shame and other stories , in 1959 . 

Though made by Ealing Studios , the film was shot at the film studios at Wembley Park in north - west London . 

It was the last feature film to be made there .
