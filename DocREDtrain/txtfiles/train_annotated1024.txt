Fabián A. González Amado ( born 1963 in Buenos Aires , Argentina ) , know with his stage name Tweety González , is an Argentine musician and record producer . 

He is best known for his work as collaborator keyboardist for the Argentine rock band Soda Stereo . 

More commonly referred to as Tweety González , he is mostly known for playing the keyboard with Soda Stereo and Fito Páez . 

Tweety has also been involved in music production and has contributed to artists such as Shakira , Gustavo Cerati , Illya Kuryaki and the Valderramas , Spinetta , Superlitio , Famasloop and several others . 

Gustavo Cerati 's 2006 Ahí vamos album — in which Tweety participated — won the Latin Grammy for Best Rock Solo Vocal Album .
