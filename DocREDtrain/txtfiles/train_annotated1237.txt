" Bože pravde " (; ; God of Justice ) is the national anthem of Serbia , as defined by the Article 7 of the Constitution of Serbia . 

" Bože pravde " was the state anthem of the Principality of Serbia and the Kingdom of Serbia until 1918 when Serbia became a part of the Kingdom of Serbs , Croats and Slovenes which was formed that year . 

It was recommended by the regional Parliament of Serbia on August 17 , 2004 and constitutionally adopted on November 8 , 2006 , after Serbia became a sovereign state again . 

The recommended text was made Law on May 11 , 2009 . 

The original song was written in 1872 with music by Davorin Jenko and lyrics by Jovan Đorđević . 

It was then a piece for the theater play Marko kazuje na kome je carstvo ( Marko names the Emperor ) , and its immense popularity with audiences prompted its adoption as the Serbian national anthem .
