Otto - Iivari Meurman ( 4 June 1890 , in Ilmajoki – 19 August 1994 , in Helsinki ) was a Finnish architect . 

He did city plans for Kauniainen and Tapiola . 

Garden city Tapiola was based on town planning of Meurman . 

Meurman resigned the Tapiola project when high houses were allowed in the area . 

Internationally garden cities do not include them . 

He wrote : Rock can be broken in short time , but you can not get it back artificially . 

Nature must be respected , and should be touched cautiously . 

By this way nature and humans can create brilliant work , that prove good national sense and civilized nature . 

( " Kallio on lyhyessä ajassa rikki räjäytetty , mutta uutta ei sen tilalle voi keinotekoisesti saada . 

Luontoa on kunnioitettava , ja siihen on kajottava varovasti . 

Silloin luonto ja ihminen voivat loihtia suurenmoisia töitä , jotka todistavat tekijänsä ja kansakunnan hyvää aistia ja korkeaa sivistystasoa . " ) 

Meurman 's concept of the nuclear family and its necessary place in society was prevalent in his career .
