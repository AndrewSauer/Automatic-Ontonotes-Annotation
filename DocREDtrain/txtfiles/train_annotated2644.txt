" Pass It On " is a song by The Coral , which was taken as the second single from their 2003 album , Magic and Medicine . 

" Pass It On " is The Coral 's highest charting single to date , reaching number five in the UK Singles Chart . 

" Pass It On " was not only released on CD and vinyl but also on DVD which contains 2 b - sides and a 3-minute documentary about the band . 

The song is similar in structure to The Beatles song " You Like Me Too Much " . 

It appeared in an episode of Scrubs , " My Turf War " and is featured in the theatrical trailer for Ken Loach 's film Looking for Eric .
