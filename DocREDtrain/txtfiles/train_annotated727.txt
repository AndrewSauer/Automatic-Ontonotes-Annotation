Traralgon ( ) is a city located in the east of the Latrobe Valley in the Gippsland region of Victoria , Australia . 

The urban population of Traralgon at the was 25,485 . 

It is the largest and fastest growing city in the greater Latrobe Valley area , which has a population of 74,170 at June 2016 and is administered by the City of Latrobe . 

The origin of the name Traralgon is uncertain . 

It is popularly believed to be derived from words from the Gunai language : tarra meaning " river " and algon meaning " little fish " . 

However , these words are not reflected in modern linguists ' knowledge of the Gunai language , where , for example , the word for river is wun wun or wurn wurn .
