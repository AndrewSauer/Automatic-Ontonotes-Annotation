She was the third child and only daughter of King George V and Queen Mary and was born during the reign of Queen Victoria , her great - grandmother . 

Mary was the paternal aunt of the current British monarch , Queen Elizabeth II . 

Her education started at home . 

World War I brought Mary out of seclusion as she launched a charity campaign to support British troops and sailors . 

She eventually became a nurse . 

Mary married Viscount Lascelles ( later the Earl of Harewood ) in 1922 . 

She was an avid collector of jewellery .
