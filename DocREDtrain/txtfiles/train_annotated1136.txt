Kiswarani ( Aymara kiswara Buddleja incana , -ni a suffix to indicate ownership , " the one with kiswara " , hispanicized spelling Quisuarani ) is a mountain in the Andes of Peru . 

Its summit reaches about above sea level . 

The mountain is located in the Cusco Region , Calca Province , Lares District , south of Lares . 

The village of Kiswarani and the Kiswarani River lie at its feet . 

The private conservation area of ' Hatun Qiwña Kiswarani Qullana ' ( Hatun Queuña Quishuarani Collana ) of the rural community of Kiswarani Qullana ( Quishuarani Collana ) lies at the mountain . 

It was founded in 2009 . 

The aim of the project is to protect the polylepis pepei . 

The small area includes the southern slope of Kiswarani in the north and the lake Qiwñaqucha in the south .
